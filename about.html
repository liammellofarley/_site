About Me

This is an archive of a website I made at 18 years old as a photography portfolio. I'm not sure how well it renders on current PCs as I haven't edited the core CSS and JS in a long time. This was made primarily as a self-motivated learning exercise and was developed in the span of a few months with minimal prior experience in web development, so I apologize if it's rough around the edges. Jekyll (https://jekyllrb.com/) was used to simplify the addition of new photos.

Outside of photography I enjoy a diverse array of hobbies, I've built several electric guitars and worked on teaching myself various instruments over the years. I've been a life long snowboarder and enjoy hiking and swimming whenever I can get the chance to leave the city. I enjoy reading, usually historical non-fiction but I also have a soft spot for science fiction with Gibson and Atwood being some of my favorite authors.
